¡Ay Van!

It is a project developed for a company that is dedicated to making trips in urbans. This project allows the company to publish the trips, informing in cards, cost, destination, time and date of departure, of each trip. In the same way, it allows the user to make the purchase of his ticket through paypal, debit or credit card.
